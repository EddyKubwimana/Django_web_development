Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where everyone, regardless of their location or socio-economic status, has access to financial services. We believe that digital financial services, like mobile money, have the potential to bring about a financial revolution by providing safe, affordable, and convenient financial services to people who have traditionally been excluded from the formal banking system. By leveraging the power of mobile technology, we aim to empower individuals and communities to take control of their financial lives and improve their financial well-being.

We recognize that financial inclusion is critical to reducing poverty and promoting economic growth, and we are committed to making it a reality. Through our digital financial services, we strive to enable people to save, borrow, and manage their money in a way that is convenient and affordable. Our vision is not only to provide access to financial services, but also to educate people on how to use them effectively, thereby promoting financial literacy and empowering them to make informed financial decisions. We believe that our vision of using digital financial services to help people access financial services will create a more inclusive and equitable world, where everyone has the opportunity to reach their full potential.

Our Mission

At Agent-Connect, our mission is to empower the unbanked population in Burundi by providing them with access to affordable, secure, and convenient financial services. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to participate in the formal financial system and benefit from the advantages it offers.

Our goal is to bridge the gap between the banked and the unbanked by leveraging technology and innovative business models to create a more inclusive financial ecosystem in Burundi. We aim to offer a range of financial services, including savings accounts, loans, and payment solutions, that meet the unique needs and preferences of the unbanked population.

We are committed to building strong relationships with our customers, understanding their financial aspirations, and providing them with the tools and resources they need to achieve their goals. Through our efforts, we hope to contribute to the development of a more resilient and prosperous Burundi, where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.
